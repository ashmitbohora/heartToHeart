Our Mission

Heart to Heart works to prevent heart disease and support those affected by it. We raise awareness of key risk factors like poor diet, inactivity, smoking, and stress, empowering healthier choices. For those already diagnosed, we provide crucial medical and emotional support. Through education, outreach, and advocacy, we strive to reduce the impact of heart disease and help people lead healthier lives.

Why it matters

Heart disease is the leading global cause of death, claiming a life every 33 seconds. It affects people from all walks of life and places a heavy financial and emotional burden on society. However, heart disease is largely preventable. By focusing on prevention and providing comprehensive support, we can change the trajectory for countless individuals, preventing pain and loss while improving the quality of life for those already living with the disease.
